// Privacy Policy

Your Music Stays
On Your Device

Morphix, published by Aleph Void LLC ("Aleph Void," "we," "us," or "our"), does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data. There is no account, no telemetry, and no analytics. The plugin makes no network requests at all — your patches, presets, and settings stay on your device. The full details are below.

// Purpose

Single Purpose

Morphix exists for one purpose only.

Morphix is a software synthesizer plugin that generates audio from MIDI input inside a host application or on its own as a standalone app.

It lets musicians play and edit a polyphonic morphing wavetable instrument from a DAW or AUv3 host — turning incoming MIDI notes and controller messages into sound, and saving and loading patches as presets. The software does not analyze your behavior, build a profile, or perform any activity unrelated to making sound.

// Data Handling

What We Handle

What we collect, transmit, and store — in plain English.

What We Collect

Nothing. Morphix does not collect your name, email, contacts, location, browsing history, credentials, financial data, health information, communications, or any usage or diagnostic data. There is no analytics SDK, no crash reporter, and no identifier of any kind.

What We Transmit

Nothing leaves your device. Morphix makes no network requests. The MIDI it receives and the audio it produces are processed locally inside the host or standalone app and never transmitted anywhere.

What We Store

On your device only: your patches and presets (as .vstpreset files where you save them) and the plugin's own preferences and UI settings, kept in standard application support locations. It is retained until you delete it or uninstall the software.

Data Sale & Sharing

We do not sell or share user data — because we do not collect any. There are no third-party SDKs, no advertising, and no service providers receiving data on our behalf.

// Permissions

Permission Justifications

Morphix requests no privacy-sensitive permissions. The items below clarify what it does and does not access.

MIDI

Morphix receives MIDI notes, controller (CC) messages, and parameter automation from your host or controller in order to play and edit the instrument. MIDI is provided to the plugin by the host; it is used solely to produce sound and is never transmitted off the device.

Audio Output

Morphix generates an audio signal and hands it back to the host (or your audio device, in standalone mode) for playback. It does not request microphone or audio-input access — it only produces output.

Local Storage

Patches, presets, and settings are written to the software's own support and configuration directories — standard application support locations on each platform. No cloud storage, no shared folders, no access to other apps' data. Files remain on the device until you delete them or uninstall the software.

Network Access

Morphix performs no network requests. There is no telemetry, no update check, no license server call, and no analytics. The plugin functions entirely offline.

Remote Code

Morphix does not execute remote code. There is no dynamic module loading, no remotely hosted scripts, and no runtime evaluation of downloaded payloads. All code is bundled inside the binaries you install.

Identifiers & Accounts

Morphix has no sign-in and creates no identifiers. We do not collect a name, email address, password, device ID, or advertising ID, and there is no user account associated with the software.

// Compliance

Developer Program Policy Compliance

Our certification under the App Store Review Guidelines and equivalent distribution policies.

We certify that Morphix's data usage complies with the Apple App Store Review Guidelines (including the App Privacy Details requirements). Specifically:

  • We do not collect any data, so the App Privacy ("Data Not Collected") declaration applies.
  • We do not sell or share user data with third parties.
  • We do not use or transfer user data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to making sound.
  • We do not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.
  • All processing — MIDI in, audio out, presets and settings — happens locally on your device and never leaves it.

Data Controller

The entity responsible for Morphix is Aleph Void LLC. We do not operate any servers that store user data, and the software does not communicate with any server.

Children's Privacy

Morphix is a tool for musicians and producers. It is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in jurisdictions where that is the applicable age). Because the software does not collect any personal information from any user, no special handling for children's data is required.

Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Similar Laws)

Privacy laws such as the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) grant users rights including access, correction, deletion, portability, and the right to object to processing of their personal data.

Because Morphix does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our systems, we hold no personal data about you to access, correct, export, or delete. All patches, presets, and preferences are stored locally on your own device, and you can view, edit, or delete them from within the software, from the system file manager, or by uninstalling it. We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA.
